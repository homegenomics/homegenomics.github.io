## What this is for

HomeGenomics is a practical handbook that helps **developers analyze their own and their family's genomic and biometric data** themselves. Instead of handing your raw test data to someone else's black box, you learn to open it, understand it, and put it to use.

It starts from one **real case**. GitLab's founder, [Sid Sijbrandij](/start/case-sid/), took his own rare cancer apart as data — the way a developer debugs a bug — designed his own treatments, and made the whole process public. We follow that case step by step to learn the pattern: **data → target → treatment**.
