## What this is for

HomeGenomics is a practical handbook that helps **developers analyze their own and their family's genomic and biometric data** themselves. Instead of handing your raw test data to someone else's black box, you learn to open it, understand it, and put it to use.

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="From raw data" icon="document">
		Start from the raw files your test returns — VCF, FASTQ, 23andMe txt — and learn what each one actually contains.
	</Card>
	<Card title="With a developer's tools" icon="laptop">
		Use Python, the CLI, and public databases to look up variants, interpret risk, and build reproducible analysis pipelines.
	</Card>
	<Card title="For your family" icon="heart">
		Relatives share DNA. One person's analysis can become a clue that protects the health of parents, children, and siblings.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Agent-friendly" icon="puzzle">
		Every page is also served at <code>/llms.txt</code>, so AI agents can read and use it directly.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
